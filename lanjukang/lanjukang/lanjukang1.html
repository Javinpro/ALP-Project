Lanjukang Island

Lanjukang Island
Makassar, South Sulawesi
Lanjukang Island, also known as Pulau Lanjukang, is a remote and serene island located about 40 kilometers from Makassar, South Sulawesi, Indonesia. It is part of the Spermonde Archipelago and is known for its pristine beaches, crystal-clear waters, and vibrant coral reefs.
