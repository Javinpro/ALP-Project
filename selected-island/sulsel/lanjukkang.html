Selat Makassar, Sulawesi Selatan

Lanjukkang Island, located in the waters of South Sulawesi, is a hidden paradise offering white sandy beaches and crystal-clear waters.
This small island is renowned for its stunning coral reefs, making it a favorite destination for snorkeling and diving. Additionally, Lanjukkang Island provides tranquility and breathtaking natural scenery, ideal for nature tourism and ecotourism. Accessible by boat from Makassar, it is perfect for a day trip, offering a serene and refreshing getaway far from the hustle and bustle of the city.
